## Art Gallery Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/16a4fe4441934676b829c4ff9bc4a762/ghb20250916) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript` etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular User, Staff, Administrator.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Personal Center (Modify Personal Information, Change Password, Change Avatar)
4. My Art Collection
5. Homepage Carousel
6. Announcement Information
7. Message Board
8. Art Museum Introduction Information
9. Art and Culture Information
10. Art Item Introduction
11. Author Information Viewing, Author Details

**Staff Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Personal Center (Modify Personal Information, Change Password, Change Avatar)
4. User Management
5. Author Management
6. Art Information Management
7. Art Collection Management
8. Announcement Information Management
9. Message Board Management
10. Introduction Management
11. Carousel Management

**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. Administrator Management
4. Staff Management
5. User Management
6. Author Management
7. Art Information Management
8. Art Collection Management
9. Announcement Information Management
10. Message Board Management
11. Introduction Management
12. Basic Data Management (Announcement Type, Introduction Type, Art Type, Author Type)
13. Carousel Management
